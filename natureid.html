🌿 About NatureID

Building tools for biodiversity through citizen science and AI

What is NatureID?

NatureID is an AI-powered nature identification platform designed for Kerala's rich biodiversity — and scalable to any region. Whether you're a researcher studying endemic species, a nature enthusiast photographing butterflies, or a curious student who found an interesting bird, NatureID helps you identify and learn about it instantly.

Built with the Western Ghats biodiversity hotspot in mind, our database includes butterflies, birds, plants, and more — with detailed information on habitat, conservation status, and local ecology.
